Who We Are

Snowy is the new kid in town for readers and book recommendations. Our mission is to help people find and share books they love.


A Few Things You Can Do On Goodreads

Track the books you're reading, have read, and want to read. Check out your personalized book recommendations. Our recommendation engine analyzes 20 billion data points to give suggestions tailored to your literary tastes. Find out if a book is a good fit for you from our community’s reviews.


A Message From The Founder

When I was in eight grade, I discovered the Feluda Mysteries written by Satyajit Ray and Tintin written by Hergé. Ever since, I've loved to read!

One day my friend texted me to send him a list of books that I have.At that moment it struck me to build Snowy.

So I decided to build a website, a place where my friends could share book reviews also can create and share bookshelves.

Snowy is that site. It is a place where you can see what your friends are reading and vice versa. You can create "bookshelves" to organize what you've read (or want to read).

Kabir Ahmed Campwala

Founder
